About Us

Fluffy Bakes' Story

"Our goal is to continue to 'make the world a smaller place' with the Caramel Cake and our other Southern specialties. We are so grateful for all of our customers and their generous and ongoing support.”

Over 15 years, Fluffy Bakes has grown leaps and bounds and we owe it all to the loyalty shown by our customers since its inception. In 2000, Fluffy Bakes production unit was acquired by Pauly and Bob and was renamed Fluffy Bakes. In December 2010, P&C was registered and rechristened as Fluffy Bakes. Our pastries have become the most soul satisfying and sought after ones for more than two decades and we continue to deliver excellence in a lip smacking and delightful way!

Delivering Freshness Every Day at a Time

We believe in the value of homemade, handcrafted quality baked goods. Not only do they warm the home, but our products will definitely warm your heart. Our favorite time of day is when our first batch comes out of the oven early in the morning, and the fresh smell fills the entire street.

Supporting Local Products

We value locally grown, fresh ingredients made into traditional recipes that taste amazing. We like to keep it simple and emphasize flavor over flash. Come check out what we’ve got prepared for you today. Your taste buds will be happy and we know you will too.
